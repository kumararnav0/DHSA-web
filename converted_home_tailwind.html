About DHSA

The Delhi Himachal Students' Association(DHSA) is a community of students hailing from the beautiful state of Himachal Pradesh, currently studying across various institutions in Delhi. Our association serves as a cultural and social platform aimed at fostering unity, promoting Himachali heritage, and providing mutual support to students far from home. Through DHSA, we organize events, cultural programs, and interactive sessions to celebrate our traditions and create a sense of belonging. Whether it's assisting new students with settling in or organizing vibrant celebrations like our annual 'Raunakein' freshers' event, DHSA is committed to building a strong network of Himachali students in the heart of Delhi. Our mission is to create a space where students can connect, collaborate, and support one another, ensuring a smoother academic journey and a stronger bond with our roots.
